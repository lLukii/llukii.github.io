> *sighs* well, that wasn't a spectacular ending

**Championship Results:** 9th place qualification rank, 2nd place think award; didn't make playoffs :/

Needless to say, these results fell short of my original hopes for the season—something along the lines of top 5 in playoffs (or near worlds qualification). In retrospect, this was a really really fucking competitive tournament—almost every alliance was scoring above 175 points. But still, I feel like our team performance was a lot sloppier than during superquals. 

Case in point, the first two qualification rounds were complete and utter disasters. I made the brilliant choice of refactoring some stuff for a driver control change right before our first match. Consequently, our auto highkey broke, and we literally didn't score a single artifact 💀. Our alliance parters were... not the best either, so we were pretty much playing solo matches in the morning. Despite our TeleOp being much faster than in superquals, it wasn't enough to offset the lead that our opposing alliance had. We ended these matches with 0 RPs (and a crashout) 💀

Luckily, we made a comeback and won the next four consecutive matches (including against Demon Dogs, but they were lowk playing a solo match that time), moving us from 31st to 9th place. Unfortunately, I proceeded to make more questionable choices during auto, such as choosing bad starting points that caused the robot to get stuck. By the time I tried to salvage it for playoffs in the pits, it was too late; no alliance captain wanted to partner with us.

We didn't win any good awards either; I mean, most of them were out of the question after judging and quals. We definetly weren't winning inspire without any crazy connections; control was out of the question with such a shitty auto, and our design was way too bulky for it to earn design or innovate. I mean, BC's insane portfolio was only enough to get us 2nd place think, so that should explain it. Well, it is a tough battlefield out there...

I guess this does leave me more time to do some other things. I've been looking into reinforcement learning recently, which seems pretty cool, so I'll do some more work on that. Also, my piano repertoire is cooked asf rn, so I need to get that going, or else my teacher will crash out on me for the 15th time this year. 

My coach also likes to say that legends are born in the offseason, so I'll lock tf in on advanced software stuff. Sounds like naive optimism, but maybe we'll embark on our NYC domination arc next year :wink:
