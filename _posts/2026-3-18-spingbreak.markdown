Of course, these are all *really* competitive programs, so I'm definitely not guaranteed a spot. Cold emailing is one of my backup plans, where I've been looking into integrating CS and Music. It's a really niche field, but it blends my two biggest passions together nicely. I'm not sure what I want to do specifically with Music and CS yet, but this thing I found on arXiv, called a Singing Voice Synthesizer, interested me a lot as a big Vocaloid fan.
