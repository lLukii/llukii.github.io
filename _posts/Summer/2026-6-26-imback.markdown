As Browning's graduation ceremony ended, I, alongside the rest of the juniors, switched spots with the graduating class on Christ Church's elaborate church podium. Cameras from gleeful parents flashed as I gazed across the long corridor leading to a faint exit. Two weeks later, my perplexed face appeared on the front of my Instagram page; it said it all: a surreal feeling, a contradictory feeling... 


*Please get me out of here*
